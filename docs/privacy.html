Privacy Policy — Draftmancer Didn't Wheel

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Draftmancer Didn't Wheel (the "Extension") is a browser extension that highlights which cards did not wheel during a live Draftmancer draft and provides a pick-by-pick replay viewer for exported draft logs. This policy explains what the Extension does and does not do with your information.

The short version

The Extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. It has no servers of its own, no analytics, and no tracking. Everything it stores stays on your own computer.

What the Extension stores

  • Draft history and viewer preferences are saved locally in your browser using the Chrome storage.local API. This data never leaves your device and is only used to show your recent drafts and remember your viewer settings. You can clear it at any time by removing the Extension or clearing its storage from chrome://extensions.

Network requests

  • Scryfall (api.scryfall.com): The Extension fetches public card data and card images from Scryfall to display cards in the replay viewer and draft overlay. These are ordinary public API requests for card information; no personal data is included in them. See the Scryfall terms for how Scryfall handles requests.
  • Draftmancer (draftmancer.com): The Extension reads draft information from the Draftmancer page you are already viewing in order to detect picks and wheels. It does not send your draft data anywhere.

What the Extension does NOT do

  • It does not collect personally identifiable information.
  • It does not use cookies, analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  • It does not transmit your draft data, browsing history, or any stored data to the developer or any third party.
  • It does not sell or share data with anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
